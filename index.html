Welcome to Wexford 👋

We hope through the use of this website, you can learn something about our beautiful county 🎉

This website was born from a community effort to promote our hometown and its vibrant history.
We hope that people who visit the site might be encouraged to explore and spend time in Wexford.
We regularly have meetups and events whch serve as educational opportunities for all. Everyone is welcome to join!

You can send us a message here to get involved.
